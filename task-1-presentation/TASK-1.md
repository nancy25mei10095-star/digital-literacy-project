## TASK-1



##### For this task, I used Canva to design a digital literacy infographic because it is easy to use and provides many ready-made templates. My infographic focuses on explaining digital literacy in a simple and practical way that students can easily understand.

##### 

##### I included topics like the meaning of digital literacy, useful tools for students, safe internet practices, and how to maintain a professional online presence. Instead of using complex definitions, I tried to present the information in short and clear points so that it looks clean and easy to read.

##### 

##### While working on this task, I realized that digital literacy is not just about using technology, but also about using it safely and responsibly. One interesting thing I noticed is that many students use digital tools daily but are not fully aware of online safety.

##### 

##### The main difficulty I faced was arranging all the information in one page without making it look crowded. Overall, this task helped me understand the importance of being aware and responsible in the digital world.
